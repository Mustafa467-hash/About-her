Overview:

Tourism as Core Segment:

Pakistan Tourism Management Company is a subsidiary of Six Sigma travels that is a well-known name in the tourism industry of Pakistan. Six Sigma as Pakistan tourism company has excelled in outbound as well as in inbound tour package sales. The company has focused a lot in the promotion of domestic tourism which is considered the back bone of the industry. The company is offering a variety of tour products to the world with cost-effective price quotes. The Tourism division of the company is fully equipped with all the latest communication facilities. The real strength of TI division of tourism is the highly skilled manpower that has the experience to handle all kind of tours whether they are cultural, adventure or tours of special interest. The company has also a team of professional multi linguist tour guides who are available on demand that is the key to success of this company. The company also specializes in all kind of adventure activities, whether one is looking for mountain trekking, mountain climbing or mountain biking.

Pakistan Tourism Management Company has focused a lot on this segment of travel industry which is an essential part of the travel needs of the consumers.
