VISA

Pakistan tourist visa page is source of information and facility for visitors to Pakistan. We are pleased to share good news about visa for Pakistan that is now much easier to get without going to Pakistan embassy anywhere in the world. You can simply click visa.nadra.gov.pk web portal that will assist you all the ay from online application, processing and to the online payment. In new online visa system the visa fees are now reduced to the lowest ever so that everyone can afford tour and Pakistan tourist visa. There is also facility of visa on arrival for fifty friendly countries that are enlisted in this web portal.

Visa for Pakistan is being offered as online e-Visa (170 countries) and processing would take 7-8 working days. With the passage of time and better orientation the processing time would be little quicker whereas electronic travel authorization (ETA) would be taken 24-48 hours prior to arrival for visa-on arrival (only for fifty friendly countries). Currently visa-on arrival facility is on trial basis limited to only five countries but later on it would be expanded to fifty more countries.

There is also good news for Sikh diaspora who somehow were not able to get nationality of the respective countries because of language tests or other reasons. They are now entitled to apply Pakistan tourist visa for yatra purpose or even for routine tourism. The pilgrims were used to have pilgrim visa only valid for three cities and fifteen days that is now open for all Pakistan the duration of each visit is for forty five days. The government of Pakistan has also reduced requirement of documents for general tourists and for pilgrims (yatrees) as well, now one do not need to gather documents such as bank statements, proof of jobs to the embassies for visa application. The applicants need only to manage a letter of invitation from a tour operator or hotel bookings for the desired Pakistan visa.
